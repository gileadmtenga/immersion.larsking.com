Pointholding: The Heart of
the Immersion

“Where the magic of science and love meet for massive transformation...”

Pointholding is a powerfully transformative approach to releasing trauma and stuck energy, with consistently profound, permanent, and liberating results. This practice involves applying sustained pressure to neurosomatic points on the body, granting access to the limbic system, the emotional command center of the brain. It's here that we can unlock and gently release what's been held inside, often for a long time. You'll lay comfortably on a massage table while two points at the base of the skull are held. If there are other pointholders present, they'll hold other specific points on your body (which correspond to specific meridian points in Chinese Medicine).

Sessions resolve old traumatic imprints, allowing you to process and permanently release past emotions and negative beliefs at the root. One of the most special things about this is how profoundly safe and easy it is to “go there,” and how the subconscious serves up exactly what you are ready for (based on your intention for the session). Pointholding offers a unique channel to the wisdom of your body, cultivating a new level of embodied connection with Self that leads to life-altering changes. I have facilitated and experienced miracles through this work. I asked Judith Johnson, one of my mentors and a pioneer of this work, if she sees this as the most powerful somatic healing modality on the planet. She responded “Yes, nothing touches it.” I sometimes call the experience an “internal power wash” for how clean and clear we often feel afterward 😊.

All those present for these sessions, including pointholders, are blessed by the process. Holding these points allows for all involved to plug into the purity of what is occurring. While trauma occurs in the darkness, pointholding sessions occur in the light and hands of a sacred container. The process alone is incredibly powerful; being witnessed and physically held in it is a gift of its own.
